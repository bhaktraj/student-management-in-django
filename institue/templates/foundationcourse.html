Foundation Course Details

JEE foundation course

We all know that preparing for JEE Main and JEE Advanced along with school studies can be a bit hectic for aspirants. So why not start their training early enough so that they do not have to face the hardships later? This is what the IIT JEE foundation course is all about. As the name suggests, a JEE foundation course is designed to lay the right basis for difficult concepts of Science and Mathematics. Whether it is an IIT foundation course for class 9 or an online IIT foundation course for class 8 or an IIT foundation course for class 6, the foundation course at our institute prepares the students to attempt a variety of competitive exams and build a strong aptitude from the beginning.
